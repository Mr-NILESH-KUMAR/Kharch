Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects the following types of information to provide and improve our expense tracking service:

  • Account information: email address, name, and password (hashed)
  • Expense data: amount, category, date, description, and payment method
  • Usage information: features accessed, time spent in app, and interaction patterns
  • Device information: browser type, operating system, and screen resolution

We do not collect sensitive financial information such as bank account numbers or credit card details.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide, maintain, and improve the Spendly service:

  • To track and categorize your expenses accurately
  • To generate spending reports and insights
  • To personalize your experience with relevant suggestions
  • To communicate important service updates and security notices
  • To improve our app based on user feedback and usage patterns
  • To prevent fraudulent activities and ensure security

We do not sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely with the following protections:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Expense data is encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt before storage
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Data backups are performed daily and stored in secure locations

Your data is stored in secure cloud infrastructure with strict access controls.

4. Third Party Services

We may use third-party services to help operate and improve Spendly:

  • Hosting providers for application deployment and data storage
  • Analytics services to understand usage patterns (anonymized data only)
  • Error monitoring services to improve app stability
  • Email services for transactional communications (password reset, etc.)

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and may only use it to provide services to us.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@spendly.example
  • Through the "Help & Support" section in the Spendly app
  • Mail: Spendly Privacy Team, 123 Finance St, New York, NY 10001

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days.
